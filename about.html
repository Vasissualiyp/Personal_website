Hello!

I'm Vasilii Pustovoit, currently navigating the vast and fascinating field of astrophysics.

My Research

primarily focuses on high-redshift simulations, using the GIZMO NBODY code to explore the early universe, particularly the formation of Population III stars and intermediate-mass black holes. These simulations are essential for understanding the fabric of our cosmos in its infancy.

Programming

is something I do in addition to my astrophysics projects. I frequently develop software tools to support my research, employing languages like C, Bash, Lua and Python to solve complex problems or analyze data. My preference for operating systems has historically been Arch, but recently I became very fond of NixOS.

Other passions

include music, specifically metal, which provides a creative outlet away from the numerical world. Additionally, board games are a favored leisure activity, offering a blend of strategy and social interaction.

Thank you

for visiting my website. I hope to share insights from my journey through astrophysics and beyond.
