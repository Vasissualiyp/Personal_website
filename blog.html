  • BLACK BOARD TALK

    NOVEMBER 8, 2024

    Title: Formation of First Stars: Distinguishing Factors

    Abstract: This talk will focus on the formation mechanisms of the Universe's first stars - the Population III stars. Deriving their properties from observed data and fundamental principles, I will discuss the role of Jeans instability at high redshifts in bridging cosmology and stellar formation. Time permitting, I will also trace the lifecycle, feedback, and the transition to Population II star formation.
